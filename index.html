What is Discball?

Discball is what you get when you combine ultimate frisbee with baseball with dodgeball. Born in the spring of 2020 by three Minnesota friends looking for a way to get together while remaining socially distant—and still hurt one another—this game has evolved into something truly special.

Whether you're slinging discs across the field or dodging incoming projectiles, Discball demands athleticism, strategy, and a healthy dose of fearlessness.

Warning: Bruises are badges of honor.
