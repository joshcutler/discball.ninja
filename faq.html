Every team develops their own style, but here are some classics:

  • The Swarm: Everyone attacks one opponent at once
  • The Shield Wall: Strong catchers up front, throwers behind
  • Chaos Theory: No plan, just throw everything everywhere
  • The Sniper: Patient, precise throws when opponents least expect it
