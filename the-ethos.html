THE WARRIOR'S WAY

The Discball Ethos

More than a game. It's a mindset. It's a community. It's a way of life.

Discball was born in Spring 2020 when three Minnesota friends asked themselves: "How can we hang out, stay socially distant, and still hurt each other?" The answer was a brilliant mashup of ultimate frisbee, baseball, and dodgeball.

Discball exists at the intersection of pure joy and fierce competition. We don't take ourselves too seriously, but we take the game very seriously. We celebrate the absurdity of hurling frisbees at each other while honoring the warrior spirit that drives us to victory.

This is our code. This is our ethos. This is Discball.

Our Core Values

Fun & Goofy

Let's be honest: we're throwing frisbees at each other. That's inherently ridiculous, and we embrace it fully.

  • •Celebrate spectacular catches with excessive enthusiasm
  • •Laugh when you get hit (then plot your revenge)
  • •Victory dances are encouraged, but keep them under 30 seconds
  • •Creative trash talk is an art form—master it

Competitive Fire

We play to win. Every throw counts. Every dodge matters. This is war (with frisbees).

  • •Give 110% effort every single round
  • •Study your opponents, learn their weaknesses
  • •Practice your throws until they're weapons
  • •Never surrender, even when you're the last one standing

Warrior Spirit

Bruises, scrapes, and close calls? They're part of the journey. We don't fear pain; we wear our battle scars with pride.

  • •A bruise is just a temporary tattoo of glory
  • •Getting hit hard? Shake it off and throw harder
  • •Respect those who take risks and pay the price
  • •Fear is natural. Courage is choosing to play anyway

Respect & Sportsmanship

We're warriors on the field, friends off it. Competition doesn't mean we lose our humanity.

  • •Call your own outs honestly—integrity is everything
  • •Help opponents up after an intense play
  • •Congratulate great plays, even from the other team
  • •Trash talk is fun; being mean is not. Know the difference

Everyone's Invited

Discball is for everyone. All skill levels, all backgrounds, all body types. If you want to play, you belong here.

  • •Welcome newcomers and teach them the ropes
  • •Celebrate different playstyles and strategies
  • •Create teams that mix experienced and new players
  • •The best warrior is the one who lifts others up

Always Evolving

Discball is young, wild, and constantly improving. We're making this up as we go, and that's beautiful.

  • •Experiment with new strategies and tactics
  • •Suggest rule tweaks that make the game better
  • •Share your best moments and epic fails
  • •Help spread Discball to new communities

The Discball Oath

"I pledge to throw with precision, dodge with grace, and compete with fire in my heart. I will honor my opponents, celebrate the absurd, and wear my bruises as badges. I am goofy yet fierce, playful yet determined. I am a Discball warrior, and I will never back down from the challenge."

Remember...

At the end of the day, Discball is about bringing people together for something ridiculous and wonderful. It's about pushing yourself, supporting your teammates, and creating memories that'll make you laugh for years to come.

Play hard. Play fair. Play Discball.
